Welcome to A Dog's Life

Founded in August 1995, by Prakesh Khan, A Dog's Life has been serving Metro Phoenix for over 20 years. Starting out in his family's shed, over the next five years Khan grew his business to include other services besides grooming. The current location of A Dog's Life opened in March 2001. Providing a whole range of services, Khan's vision has been realized.

A Message from Prakesh Khan
