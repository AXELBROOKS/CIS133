Web Hosting Companies

For this assignment, the three web hosting companies I reviewed were HostPapa, Bluehost, and HostGator. These three were the top three hostingreview.com reviewed. Here is what I found:

Company Link # of Websites Allowed SSD Storage Domain Registration PHP and mySQL Support E-Commerce platform Total Cost
www.hostpapa.com 1 website 100GB Free domain registration Yes LoadedCommerce $3.95/month
www.bluehost.com 1 website 40GB Free domain registration Yes WooCommerce/ YITH $6.75/month
www.hostgator.com 1 website 10GB Free domain registration Yes Magento/ Prestashop/ Zencart $3.75/month
NOTE: Both Bluehost and HostGator offer free domain registration for the first year with the following years having a cost that varies depending on the TLD.
NOTE: All of these websites have an E-Commerce package that includes web hosting therefore it is impossible to seperate the cost into the three categories of the instructions.

Based off the research I conducted, if I had to pick one of these three web hosting sites, I would probably pick HostPapa. It offers the most storage, free domain registration and offers PHP and mySQL support as well as being a reasonable cost.
